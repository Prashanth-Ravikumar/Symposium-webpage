About Pravartan '25

Pravartan'25 is the first-ever national-level technical symposium hosted by the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) at Adithya Institute of Technology, under the banner of ELECTRA, our official student association. Pravartan'25 represents a significant milestone for the department, aiming to bring together bright minds from across the nation to participate in a variety of technical and non-technical events that foster creativity, innovation, and collaboration.

As the flagship event of ELECTRA, Pravartan'25 is a platform where students can showcase their technical prowess, share ideas, and engage with experts in the field of electrical and electronics engineering. This symposium will feature a range of activities designed to challenge participants' skills, including hands-on competitions, thought-provoking presentations, and interactive sessions.

ELECTRA, the driving force behind this symposium, is dedicated to providing opportunities for students to grow both academically and professionally. With initiatives like Pravartan'25, ELECTRA seeks to inspire the next generation of engineers to push the boundaries of technology and innovation.
